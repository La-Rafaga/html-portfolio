The Best Movies According to Sebastian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lord of the rings (The whole trilogy)

Possibly the best fantasy books ever written. One odf the best adaptations with cool characters and insane world building.

Fight Club

Deep movie with a very heavy yet relevant message.

Dune (latest movies)

Amazing book and films about a sci-fi dystopian world. it inspired the star wars universe, which I also like.
